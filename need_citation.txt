Their music has grown in popularity due to their busking performances on Queen Street Mall and originals uploaded to YouTube.
In the band's earlier years, The Fergies gained recognition by winning a number of local, state and national band and songwriting competitions and awards.
The Fergies is a folk/indie/rock/pop band from Brisbane, Australia formed by the five Ferguson siblings, Kahlia, Daniel, Joel, Nathan, and Shani.
